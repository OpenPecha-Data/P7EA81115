asyāṃ khalu punar acintyatāyām atulyatāyām aprameyatāyām asaṃkhyeyatāyām asamasamatāyāṃ bhāṣyamāṇāyāṃ
pañcānāṃ bhikṣuśatānām anupādāya āsravebhyaś cittāni vimuktāni, viṃśateś ca bhikṣuṇīśatānām
anupādāya āsravebhyaś cittāni vimuktāni, ṣaṣṭeś copāsakaśatānāṃ virajo vigatamalaṃ
dharmeṣu dharmacakṣur viśuddham, triṃśateś copāsikānāṃ virajo vigatamalaṃ dharmeṣu dharmacakṣur
viśuddham, viṃśatyā ca bodhisattvair anutpattikeṣu dharmeṣu kṣāntiḥ pratilabdhābhūt
|te ca bhagavatā ihaiva bhadrakalpe vyākṛtā anuttarāyāṃ samyaksaṃbodhau |ye ’pi te upāsakopāsikāś
ca yeṣāṃ virajo vigatamalaṃ dharmeṣu dharmacakṣur viśuddham, te ’pi bhagavatā
vyākṛtāḥ |teṣām apy anupādāyāsravebhyaś cittaṃ vimokṣyate ||
