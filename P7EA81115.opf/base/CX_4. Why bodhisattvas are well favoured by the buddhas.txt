evam ukte bhagavān āyuṣmantaṃ śāriputram etad avocat - sādhu sādhu śāriputra |idam api te śāriputra
buddhānubhāvena pratibhāti |atha khalv āyuṣmān subhūtir bhagavantam etad avocat -
āścaryaṃ bhagavan yāvat suparigṛhītāś ca suparīttāś ca suparīnditāś ca ime bodhisattvā mahāsattvās
tathāgatenārhatā samyaksaṃbuddhena |bhagavān āha - tathā hi te subhūte bodhisattvā
mahāsattvā bahujanahitāya (109) pratipannā bahujanasukhāya lokānukampāyai mahato janakāyasyārthāya
hitāya sukhāya devānāṃ ca manuṣyāṇāṃ ca |anukampakā anukampām upādāyānuttarāṃ samyaksaṃbodhim
abhisaṃboddhukāmāḥ |anuttarāṃ samyaksaṃbodhim abhisaṃbudhyānuttaraṃ dharmaṃ
deśayitukāmāḥ ||
