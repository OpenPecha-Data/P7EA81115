evaṃ hi subhūte prajñāpāramitāyāṃ śikṣamāṇena bodhisattvena mahāsattvena sarvāḥ pāramitā saṃgṛhītā
bhavanti, sarvāḥ pāramitodgṛhītā bhavanti, sarvāḥ pāramitā anugatā bhavanti |sarvāḥ
pāramitā antargatā bhavanti |tadyathāpi nāma subhūte satkāyadṛṣṭau dvāṣaṣṭidṛṣṭigatāny antargatāni
bhavanti, evam eva subhūte prajñāpāramitāyāṃ śikṣamāṇasya bodhisattvasya mahāsattvasya
tasyāṃ sarvāḥ pāramitā antargatā bhavanti |tadyathāpi nāma subhūte puruṣasya jīvitendriye
pravartamāne sarvāṇīndriyāṇy antargatāni bhavanti, evam eva subhūte bodhisattvasya
mahāsattvasya prajñāpāramitāyāṃ śikṣamāṇasya sarvakuśalā dharmā antargatā bhavanti |tadyathāpi
nāma subhūte puruṣasya jīvitendriye niruddhe sarvāṇīndriyāṇi niruddhāni bhavanti, evam
eva subhūte bodhisattvasya mahāsattvasya prajñāpāramitāyāṃ śikṣamāṇasyājñāne niruddhe sarve
’kuśalā dharmā niruddhā bhavanti, sarvāś ca tadanyāḥ pāramitā antargatāḥ parigṛhītā bhavanti
